What is neuroticism?

Neuroticism is one of the five main personality traits of the Big Five model. It is defined as "the propensity to experience negative emotions" [1 https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4382368/] and therefore with feelings such as anxiety, self-doubt, depression,...,. It as well leads to numerous common mental disorders such as substance use disorder, anxiety disorders and mood disorders.
